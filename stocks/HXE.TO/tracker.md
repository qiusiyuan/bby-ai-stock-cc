# HXE.TO — Global X S&P/TSX Capped Energy Index ETF

> **What it is.** HXE is a corporate-class ETF managed by Global X Canada, tracking the S&P/TSX Capped Energy Index — a market-cap-weighted index of Canadian energy companies listed on the TSX, with individual stock weights capped at 25%. Priced in CAD on the Toronto Stock Exchange. The corporate-class structure offers a Canadian-specific tax advantage: return of capital is generated rather than taxable dividends. Expense ratio: ~0.28% (MER; confirm at Global X Canada).

## Risks specific to this product
Currency risk: fund priced in CAD; CAD/USD moves add or subtract return for non-CAD base investors. Oil price risk: the entire fund moves with crude (WTI and WCS). Geopolitical risk in two directions: Middle East supply (Iran deal is a near-term headwind for oil prices) and Canadian pipeline politics (Trans Mountain capacity, export terminals). Volume is thin (~2,200–3,000 shares/day) — this is an illiquid ETF; use limit orders. No US energy diversification.
