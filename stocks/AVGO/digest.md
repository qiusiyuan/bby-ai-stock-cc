# AVGO — Broadcom digest

> **One-line thesis.** The quiet compounder of AI infrastructure: custom ASICs for hyperscalers (Google TPU, Meta MTIA) at premium margins plus VMware as a durable software cash machine — *wrong if* hyperscalers bring custom silicon design in-house at scale, or VMware enterprise churn accelerates materially.

## What it does

Two businesses under one roof: custom AI accelerators (XPUs) and networking ASICs for hyperscalers, plus VMware private cloud software (VCF, vSphere, NSX). The XPU business is the AI growth engine; VMware provides FCF stability. Hock Tan runs it as a serial cash machine.
