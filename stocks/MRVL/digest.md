## What it does

Marvell designs data infrastructure chips: custom AI accelerators (XPUs) for hyperscalers (primarily AWS Trainium), high-speed optical interconnect (SerDes, silicon photonics, co-packaged optics for AI cluster fabrics), and traditional storage/networking controllers. Fabless — TSMC manufactures. S&P 500 inclusion imminent.
