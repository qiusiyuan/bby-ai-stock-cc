# GOOG — Alphabet digest

> **One-line thesis.** Owns Search — the highest-intent ad surface ever built — and is defending it with Gemini AI while building the world's #2 cloud platform — *wrong if* AI-native search erodes query volume materially, or DOJ forces structural separation of distribution assets.

## What it does

Google Search, YouTube, Gmail, Maps, Chrome, Android, and Google Cloud — plus Waymo and DeepMind. Advertising is ~$300B of revenue; Cloud is the fast-growing second engine. Gemini is being embedded across all products as the AI integration layer.
