> **One-line thesis.** Snowflake is either the AI data layer every enterprise AI initiative runs through (Cortex AI + OpenAI partnership), or an expensive data warehouse getting outflanked by Databricks — *wrong if* consumption growth stalls below 20%, NRR breaks under 115%, or a major enterprise visibly migrates away.

## What it does

Multi-cloud data platform (AWS/Azure/GCP) charging on compute consumption. Enterprises store, process, and now run AI workloads on Snowflake. Key differentiators: Data Marketplace (data sharing ecosystem), Cortex AI (native LLM functions), Snowpark (code in Python/Java inside the warehouse), and OpenAI partnership for enterprise agentic AI.
