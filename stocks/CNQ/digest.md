# CNQ — Canadian Natural Resources digest

> **One-line thesis.** Capital-allocation machine in oil sands: long-life low-decline assets generate durable FCF through the cycle — *wrong if* WTI stays below $50 for 2+ years, Canada imposes production caps, or management makes a large dilutive acquisition.

## What it does

Canada's largest independent oil and gas producer, with 30–50 year oil sands reserve life and extremely low natural decline rates. Produces SCO (synthetic crude), thermal bitumen, and natural gas from Western Canada, plus modest North Sea and West Africa volumes.
