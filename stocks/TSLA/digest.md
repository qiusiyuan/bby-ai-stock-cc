# TSLA — Tesla, Inc. digest

> **One-line thesis.** The entire bull case is the autonomy option — FSD/Robotaxi monetization by 2027-28 — *wrong if* FSD fails regulatory approval in major markets, Robotaxi slips beyond 2028, or Musk's political exposure causes irreversible brand damage.

## What it does

Designs and manufactures BEVs (Model 3/Y/S/X/Cybertruck) and grid-scale energy storage (Megapack), and is developing a Robotaxi/Cybercab autonomous fleet. FSD software subscription is the bridge between the car business and the autonomy business. The data flywheel from 6M+ vehicles is its core long-term moat claim.
