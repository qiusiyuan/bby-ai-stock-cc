## What it does

AMD designs CPUs (EPYC for servers, Ryzen for PCs) and GPUs (MI-series AI accelerators, Radeon) sold to hyperscalers, OEMs, and gamers. TSMC manufactures all leading-edge parts. The Xilinx acquisition added FPGAs; the MEXT acquisition (June 2026) adds AI memory optimization software for data center stacks.
