## What it does

Three segments: CCG (PC/laptop CPUs — legacy cash cow, losing share), DCAI (server CPUs + Gaudi AI accelerators — mixed results), and Intel Foundry (contract manufacturing — the turnaround thesis). CEO Lip-Bu Tan is pivoting Intel toward foundry services, and Trump's announcement that Apple will design and build chips with Intel is the first major commercial validation.
