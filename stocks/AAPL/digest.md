## What it does

Sells iPhones, Macs, iPads, and wearables to 2.3B active devices worldwide, and monetizes that installed base through the App Store (30% cut), iCloud, Apple Music, Apple TV+, Apple Pay, and advertising. Services is the compounding business; hardware is the ecosystem entry point.
