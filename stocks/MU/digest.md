## What it does

Designs and manufactures DRAM, NAND, and HBM (high-bandwidth memory). HBM is stacked DRAM used in AI accelerators like NVIDIA H100/H200/Blackwell; it's the high-margin, supply-constrained product driving the bull case. Conventional DRAM and NAND serve PCs, smartphones, data centers, and automotive markets.
