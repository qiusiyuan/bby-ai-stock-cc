> **One-line thesis.** The highest-quality US-listed integrated major — Permian scale, 36-year dividend growth streak, and Guyana optionality — returning 7-8% of market cap annually while the Hess arbitration overhang creates a mis-priced entry — *wrong if* Hess arbitration is lost and WTI breaks below $50/bbl simultaneously.

## What it does

Global integrated energy major. Upstream (Permian Basin, Tengiz/Kazakhstan, Gulf of Mexico, Australia LNG) + Downstream (refining, chemicals, fuel retail) + All Other. Hess acquisition ($53B pending) adds Guyana Stabroek block — potentially one of the best oil assets of the 2020s.
