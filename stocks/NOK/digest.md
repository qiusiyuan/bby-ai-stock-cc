## What it does

Nokia sells B2B network infrastructure: 5G radio access equipment for carriers, fiber/optical/IP transport for telcos and data centers, private wireless networks, and 5G core software. Nokia Technologies licenses its patent portfolio (4G/5G SEPs). Nokia does not sell phones — the Nokia brand on consumer handsets is licensed to others.
