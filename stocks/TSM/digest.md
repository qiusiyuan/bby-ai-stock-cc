# TSM — Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company digest

> **One-line thesis.** The irreplaceable chokepoint of the AI hardware stack — every advanced AI chip runs through TSMC fabs and the 2nm/1.4nm lead widens each year — *wrong if* Taiwan Strait escalation triggers customer diversification, Intel closes the process gap in 3–4 years, or AI hardware spending plateaus before Arizona/Japan fabs de-risk the concentration.

## What it does

Pure-play semiconductor foundry: manufactures chips designed by Apple (~25% revenue), NVIDIA, AMD, Qualcomm, Broadcom, and hundreds of others. At the most advanced nodes (3nm, 2nm, and forthcoming 1.4nm), TSMC has no credible peer on yield and customer trust.
