> **One-line thesis.** Compounds at >30% revenue growth through 2027 on AI data-center dominance + CUDA switching costs — *wrong if* hyperscaler custom silicon erodes GPU wallet share, or if the AI capex cycle reverses sharply.

## What it does

Designs AI accelerator GPUs (H100/H200/Blackwell) that are the dominant platform for training and deploying large language models, and runs the CUDA software ecosystem that locks in 4M+ developers. Data centers — not gaming — are now the business.
