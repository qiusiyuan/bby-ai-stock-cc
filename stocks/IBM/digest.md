# IBM — International Business Machines digest

> **One-line thesis.** Steady compounder at mid-single-digit growth with 5%+ FCF yield and a WatsonX AI re-rate option — *wrong if* WatsonX fails against Microsoft/Google in enterprise AI, or if consulting revenue declines as AI automates professional services.

## Snapshot

```kpi
Price | $249.10 | -5.05% today; -9% in 5 days; near 50-day MA
Market cap | $234B | modest for enterprise IT at 3.8x sales
Revenue (2025) | ~$62B | software 40%, consulting 35%, infrastructure 20%
FCF (TTM) | $12.2B | ~5.2% yield at current price — strongest in large-cap IT
```

## What it does

Runs enterprise hybrid-cloud infrastructure (Red Hat OpenShift) and AI consulting, and sells WatsonX as the governance/workflow AI platform for regulated industries. 264K consultants embedded in banks, insurers, and governments globally make IBM extremely sticky.

## Outlook

```scenarios
Bull | 20 | $320 | WatsonX wins enterprise AI platform deals; software re-rates to 25-28x earnings
Base | 55 | $270 | Software 5-7% growth (Red Hat-led); consulting flat; FCF steady; multiple holds
Bear | 25 | $190 | AI automates consulting; WatsonX loses to MSFT/Google; FCF disappoints
```
