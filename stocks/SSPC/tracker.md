# SSPC — Leverage Shares 2X Short SPCX Daily ETF

> **What it is.** SSPC is a **2x daily inverse** ETF issued by Leverage Shares (Themes ETF Trust), launched June 15, 2026, targeting -200% of SPCX's (SpaceX) daily price return. The fund rebalances its derivative exposure every single trading day to reset the 2x leverage. **Compounding decay is unavoidable**: daily rebalancing means multi-day returns diverge significantly from -2x the SPCX cumulative return, and in volatile or trending markets, the fund will lose value faster than the math of -2x implies. This is a short-term trading instrument, not a hedge to hold. Expense ratio: 0.75%. Inception: June 15, 2026 — fewer than 5 trading days of history as of this add.

## Risks specific to this product
**Decay risk is the defining risk.** Leverage Shares' prospectus explicitly states that SSPC is designed for traders with a single-day holding period. Multi-day holding in any trending or volatile environment will produce returns materially worse than -2x SPCX's cumulative move. Today's +7.33% happened because SPCX declined — but in SPCX's current bull run, every up day in SpaceX erodes SSPC through daily rebalance losses. The fund is brand-new (June 15, 2026) with no established AUM figure and very limited price history — execution risk and wide bid/ask spreads are real. Single-stock exposure to SPCX means Musk-specific events (governance, personal controversy) can trigger extreme intraday moves that reset against the position. The 0.75% expense ratio accelerates drag over time.
