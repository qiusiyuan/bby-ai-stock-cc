## What it does

Operates three compounding businesses: e-commerce + marketplace (the flywheel), AWS cloud computing with AI services (the profit engine, ~38% operating margin), and digital advertising (the third-largest platform globally). Each feeds the others through Prime data, logistics, and developer ecosystems.
