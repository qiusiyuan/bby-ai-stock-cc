## What it does

Runs Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger, and Threads — 3.35 billion people daily. Revenue is 97%+ advertising; the AI layer (Meta AI, ad ranking improvements, Ray-Ban glasses) is the next monetization surface.
