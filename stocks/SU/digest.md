## What it does

Canada's largest integrated oil company. Mines and extracts bitumen from Alberta's Athabasca oil sands, upgrades it to synthetic crude, refines it, and sells it via ~1,500 Petro-Canada retail stations. Vertical integration cushions earnings when crude prices are volatile.
