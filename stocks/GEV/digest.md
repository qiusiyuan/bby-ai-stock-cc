# GEV — GE Vernova digest

> **One-line thesis.** The sole at-scale supplier of gas turbines and grid infrastructure at the exact moment AI-driven power demand breaks a 20-year electricity consumption plateau — *wrong if* hyperscaler capex retreats, Power margins fail to expand above mid-teens, or Wind losses persist beyond 2026.

## What it does

Makes gas turbines (Power, ~7,000 units globally), grid hardware — transformers, substations, power conversion (Electrification), and wind turbines (Wind). Power and Electrification are the profitable growth engine; Wind is the restructuring story management is working to close.
